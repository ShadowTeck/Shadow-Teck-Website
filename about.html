ABOUT

Started in 2017, Shadow Teck became a multimedia company selling services and products to those who thirst creativity. "Shadow" (Shadow Teck's Founder & CEO) is a photographer, videographer, web developer, and creative entrepreneur. Started at age 12, Shadow began his creative persuit to learn as much as he could and express the world around his in his own creative way. Shadow started his YouTube channel in 2017 which marked the beginning of his busniess. He created mostly tech videos but as the channel grew he began to make more diverse content that he enjoy making just as much as releasing. In early 2019, Shadow made his first FreeCodeCamp tutorial on "Bulma CSS" which is ironically enough the css framework for this very website! The video has now racked up over 35,000 views on a channel with over 1 Million subscribers. After this small success Shadow decided that he would take the bussniess more seriously and decided to sell his brand "Shadow Teck" as a merch line, while at the same time offer the services he enjoys doing. Shadow now produces many multiple forms of media such as Podcasts, YouTube videos, Blogs, Social media and more. Along with still working on other side projects including Programming, Web, Film and Photography. It's been a long journey, but Shadow still has many plans for the future!
